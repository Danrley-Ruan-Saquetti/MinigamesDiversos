Controls

  • Left click to move.
  • Right click OR Spacebar to stab.

Tips

  • Kill everyone you think is a real person!
  • Stabbing costs 50 stamina during the day.
  • Stabbing costs 25 stamina during the night.
  • Your move speed is based on your stamina.
  • Stabbing a player at the same time they stab you results in a parry where no one gets killed.
  • Kill your target for extra bonus score.
